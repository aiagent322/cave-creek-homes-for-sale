Cave Creek Desert Foothills & Custom Estates

North of downtown Cave Creek, the residential market shifts dramatically. Quarter-acre master-planned lots give way to 2 to 5-acre custom parcels with Black Mountain views, private wells and septic systems, and an absence of HOA restrictions. This is Cave Creek's trophy residential tier — custom-designed homes, resort pools, casitas, and workshop buildings on land that simply cannot be replicated at this proximity to Scottsdale and Phoenix.

The Properties

Custom estate homes in the Cave Creek foothills typically range from 2,500 to 6,000+ sq ft on lots of 1 to 5 acres. Many properties have been custom-built by their original owners, which means architectural variety and quality range widely — standard due diligence includes structure, roof, and site inspections beyond what a suburban home requires. Private well and septic are standard on most foothill parcels. Water quality testing and a pump test are baseline requirements before closing.

Prices

Foothills custom estate homes typically start at $1.5M and extend well above $5M for exceptional view lots with premium construction. Price per acre varies significantly based on elevation, view corridor, well performance, and building quality. These properties are the highest insurance premium tier in Cave Creek — wildfire risk zones affect many foothill addresses, and coverage availability has tightened significantly since 2023. Research insurance options before going under contract.

Jurisdiction Note

Many foothill parcels carry a Cave Creek mailing address but are in unincorporated Maricopa County rather than the incorporated Town of Cave Creek. The rules differ — setbacks, permitted accessory uses, and lighting requirements vary between Town and County jurisdiction. Confirm the parcel's exact jurisdiction from the Maricopa County Assessor before writing.
