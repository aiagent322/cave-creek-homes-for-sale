Dove Valley Ranch — Cave Creek

Dove Valley Ranch is an 850-acre master-planned gated community in Cave Creek, built around the Dove Valley Ranch Golf Club — an 18-hole public course designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr. It is one of Cave Creek's most complete residential communities: gated entry, walkable paths and parks, family-friendly amenities, and proximity to both Old Town Cave Creek and North Scottsdale, all while maintaining the dark-sky character Cave Creek is known for. Street lighting is limited to intersections, so stars are visible from the patio.

The Community

Dove Valley Ranch includes single-family homes, condominiums, and townhomes spread across several sub-neighborhoods including Emerald Greens (backing to the back nine), Arroyo Trails (smaller homes with modern amenities), and various others. The golf course is the community centerpiece — because it is public rather than private, there are no mandatory club membership costs for residents. The Dove Valley Ranch Golf Club has annual pass options and accepts daily green fee play, making it accessible without a commitment. Amenities within the community include playgrounds, green spaces, sidewalks, and community parks.

Location & Commute

Dove Valley Ranch is located off Cave Creek Road, approximately 5 miles south of downtown Cave Creek. Drive time to Old Town Cave Creek dining and shopping is under 10 minutes. North Scottsdale amenities at Desert Ridge and Kierland are approximately 20 minutes south. The Loop 101 freeway access is roughly 15–20 minutes via Tatum Boulevard. Phoenix Sky Harbor is approximately 35–40 minutes in normal traffic.

Prices & Product

Single-family homes in Dove Valley Ranch typically range from the mid-$500Ks for smaller non-golf-view properties to $1.2M+ for larger homes with premium fairway, mountain, or lake views. Condos within the community start in the $400Ks. Home sizes range broadly — smaller patio-style homes to 3,000+ sq ft estate-style properties. Most homes were built in the 1990s–2000s with substantial renovation activity in recent years.

Schools

Dove Valley Ranch is served by Cave Creek Unified School District (B+, Niche). The specific feeder schools are Lone Mountain Elementary School, Sonoran Trails Middle School, and Cactus Shadows High School. Confirm enrollment eligibility with the district for any specific address before writing.
