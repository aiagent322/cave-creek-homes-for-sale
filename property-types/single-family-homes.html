Cave Creek Single-Family Homes For Sale

Single-family homes are the dominant product in Cave Creek's residential market, spanning from quarter-acre lots in Tatum Ranch's established 1990s–2000s sub-neighborhoods to larger estate lots in the Cave Creek foothills. Median sold price across the 85331 zip was approximately $815K in early 2026. The full range runs from the $600Ks in Tatum Ranch's SFR tier to $1.2M and above in golf course–adjacent and foothill communities — with custom acreage estates taking the market well above $2M.

Tatum Ranch Single-Family Homes

Tatum Ranch contains the largest concentration of single-family homes in Cave Creek — 36 sub-neighborhoods built by 12 different builders from the late 1980s through the early 2000s. Lot sizes range from quarter-acre to one-acre. Twelve different builders means real architectural variety: Mediterranean stucco next to desert contemporary next to traditional ranch. Established 1990s sub-neighborhoods typically trade $600K–$900K; newer gated enclaves and premium lots command $900K–$1.2M. Cave Creek Unified School District (B+) serves every Tatum Ranch address.

Dove Valley Ranch Single-Family Homes

Dove Valley Ranch is a gated Sonoran Desert community built around the public Dove Valley Ranch Golf Club. Single-family homes range from approximately $550K for non-course-view properties to $1.2M+ for premium golf course or mountain view lots. The community is known for dark skies (street lights only at intersections), walking paths, playgrounds, and access to Cave Creek Unified schools. Drive time to downtown Cave Creek is under 10 minutes; Scottsdale is approximately 20 minutes south.

Rancho Manana & Old Town Area

Older established neighborhoods adjacent to the Rancho Manana Golf Club and Cave Creek's Old Town core typically feature larger lots, mature landscaping, and homes built from the 1970s through 1990s with substantial renovation history. Prices range $700K–$1.5M depending on lot size, condition, and course proximity. Walking distance to Cave Creek Road dining and shopping is a distinguishing feature not available in the more southern communities.

What to Check Before You Write

In Cave Creek, jurisdiction matters more than the mailing address. Confirm whether a specific parcel is inside the Town of Cave Creek or in unincorporated Maricopa County — the rules on setbacks, permitted uses, and lighting differ. HOA layers are common in Tatum Ranch and the gated communities; confirm master HOA and sub-HOA dues and CC&Rs. Wildfire insurance availability should be verified for any property adjacent to desert open space before going under contract.
